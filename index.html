how to prevent virus

Alcohol in-activates germs like viruses by attacking their cell structures. For example, alcohol damages the lipid layer that envelops the novel coronavirus. That makes the virus impossible to infect anyone.

maintain distance

maintain social distances, as in public places we never know who is infected. Always mainatin a distance of 6 feet.

wash your hand

Handwashing is one of the best ways to protect yourself and your family from getting sick.Washing hands can keep you healthy and prevent the spread of covid-19.

wear a mask

Always wear mask to prevent form covid-19 virus. Now a days experts are recomending to wear double mask as the new strain of covid-19 is more dangerous.
